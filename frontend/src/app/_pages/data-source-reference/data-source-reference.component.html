GDPR
Compliance

We prioritize the privacy and data protection of our users, development and data science communities. We want to assure you that we are fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).


All the data we use on our platform is sourced from publicly available information and can be obtained from the official website of the Brazilian government. By utilizing openly accessible data, we ensure that our operations adhere to the GDPR guidelines while providing valuable insights to our users.


For more information about the government's open data initiative, you can visit the official website at https://www.gov.br/governodigital/pt-br/dados-abertos. If you have any concerns or questions regarding your personal data or our GDPR compliance, please don't hesitate to contact us
